About

Born in the 90s, I've been passionate about video games, since I played Sonic The Hedgehog on Genesis.

My favorite video game era is the 64bit because of all the weird and funny gameplay ideas that emerged.

This is why I always try to design weird original mechanics in my projects.

Due to my diverse experience, I can be a link between all the different individuals in your studio.

So if you need polyvalent game designer in your team, I might be the one you're looking for.

Feel free to contact me at: antoinesemblat@hotmail.fr
